Overview

Discover the best of Kashmir with our 6 Nights / 7 Days Jammu to Jammu Package. This itinerary combines iconic sights with hidden gems, offering an enriching travel experience. Start your journey from Jammu, continue to Srinagar’s lakes and gardens, enjoy excursions to Sonmarg, Gulmarg, and the lesser-known paradise of Doodhpathri, and wrap up your trip with a peaceful stay in Pahalgam.

With a blend of natural beauty, cultural landmarks, and unique experiences like a traditional houseboat stay, this package is ideal for families, couples, and groups who want to explore Kashmir in depth.

Itinerary

DAY 1: Pickup from Jammu → Srinagar

Meet our representative in Jammu and drive to Srinagar (approx. 270 km). Enjoy the scenic mountain roads and lush valleys along the way. On arrival, check in to your Srinagar hotel and rest after the journey.

Overnight stay in Srinagar hotel.

DAY 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

After breakfast, begin your Srinagar tour:

- Visit Mughal Gardens – Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi.

- Visit Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic views.

- Evening: Enjoy a Shikara ride on Dal Lake.

Overnight stay in Srinagar hotel.

DAY 3: Day Excursion to Sonmarg

Post breakfast, drive to Sonmarg (approx. 80 km). Surrounded by snow-clad mountains and glaciers, Sonmarg is called the Meadow of Gold.

Optional activities (own cost):

- Pony ride to Thajiwas Glacier.

- Trekking and seasonal snow activities.

Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

DAY 4: Day Trip to Gulmarg

After breakfast, head towards Gulmarg (approx. 50 km). Highlights include:

- Ride the famous Gulmarg Gondola for panoramic views.

- Visit St. Mary’s Church and Maharani Temple.

- Optional winter activities: skiing, snowboarding, or sledging.

Return to Srinagar in the evening. Overnight stay in Srinagar hotel.

DAY 5: Day Trip to Doodhpathri

After breakfast, drive to Doodhpathri (approx. 45 km from Srinagar). A lesser-known gem, Doodhpathri is known as the Valley of Milk due to its pristine streams and lush meadows.

- Enjoy nature walks, photography, and pony rides.

- Relax by the river and breathe in the fresh mountain air.

Return to Srinagar in the evening and check into a traditional houseboat on Dal Lake. Overnight stay in Srinagar houseboat.

DAY 6: Srinagar → Pahalgam (Overnight Stay)

Check out after breakfast and drive to Pahalgam (approx. 90 km). En route, visit saffron fields at Pampore and the Awantipura ruins.

In Pahalgam, explore:

- Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari (optional entry/activities).

- Enjoy the Lidder River and surrounding pine forests.

Overnight stay in Pahalgam hotel.

DAY 7: Pahalgam → Jammu Drop

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and drive back to Jammu (approx. 250 km). Tour concludes with lasting memories of Kashmir.
